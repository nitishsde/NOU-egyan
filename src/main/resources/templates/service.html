FEATURES __________________

STUDENT SERVICES

Login

Make your way into the portal through the login page where different users are provided with a username and password to gain access to university programs and learning materials.

Dashboard

The AI-based dashboard serves as the administrative home page showing course details, assignments, quizzes, self-learning material, notifications and help options.

Self Learning Material

Students have access to videos, PDFs, images, assignments, quizzes and links for complete online learning of enrolled courses.

e-Books

E-Books give digital access to notes, videos, PDFs, presentations and self-assessment tools on a single platform.

Self-Assessment Tools

Students can evaluate their progress through quizzes, assignments, videos and online learning resources.

Performance

Students can track their academic performance and progress across certificate, diploma and degree programs.

Feedback

Students can give feedback to improve courses and self-learning material.

Course Catalogue

The course catalogue lists all courses with details about duration, credits, modules and prerequisites.
